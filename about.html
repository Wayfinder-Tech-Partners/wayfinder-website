About Us

IT management the way it should be.

We started Wayfinder because too many businesses were being underserved by IT providers who over-promised, under-delivered, and made pricing a mystery.

Our Mission

Enterprise-grade IT for businesses that can't afford enterprise IT teams.

Most small and mid-sized businesses face a familiar dilemma: they need serious IT infrastructure and security, but they don't have the budget or headcount to build an internal team capable of managing it.

Wayfinder exists to solve that. We bring the tools, processes, and expertise of a large enterprise IT department to businesses of any size — at a price that makes sense.

We build on Microsoft 365 because it's the platform your team already knows, and because it gives us the most powerful foundation to layer security, compliance, and management on top of.

How We Work

Straightforward from day one

Every engagement starts with a consultation — a chance for us to understand your environment before we commit to serving it.

01

Consultation

We spend time learning about your business, your existing tools, your team, and your goals. Basic and Standard require one month of precontract consultation; Premium requires three. This is how we make sure we can actually deliver on our commitments.

02

Onboarding

We treat onboarding as a project — with a defined scope, timeline, and set of deliverables. Any remediation steps identified during onboarding become additional pre-identified projects, so there are no surprises.

03

Ongoing Partnership

Regular reviews keep us aligned with your business — quarterly for Basic, monthly for Standard, weekly for Premium. We're not just a help desk. We're invested in where your business is going.

Our Values

What we stand for

Transparency

02

Accountability

We define SLOs and hold ourselves to them. If we say 4-hour response, we mean 4-hour response. Our performance is measurable and visible to you.

Our Approach to Partnerships

We know when to bring in the right people

Work Subcontracting

When your primary Wayfinder contact is unavailable and work is time-sensitive, we subcontract to a trusted technical resource. They work under our agreement — you don't need a new relationship.

Service Partnerships

When a client needs escalated services beyond our scope, we bring in a vetted service partner — under a Business Associate Agreement governed by our master contract.

Relationship Referrals

For needs that no MSP can fulfill, we refer trusted providers. In this case, no contractual obligations exist between the referred party and Wayfinder — we're simply connecting you with the right people.

Standalone Consulting

Not ready for a managed services agreement? Professional services are available as standalone engagements — 5–15 hours/month at $300/hour, or as a scoped Assessment (5–30 hours/month) at $350/hour. Plus costs where applicable.
